Potato Casserole

Description

This potato casserole is a family favorite. We enjoy it during every holiday and it has become such a treasure to me that I've memorized the recipe. This recipe has gotten me through some very difficult times. In fact, I am tempted to make it now.

What makes this recipe extra special is that we add Corn Flakes on the top to give it an extra crunch. By the way: Definitely DO NOT use Frosted Flakes! Also, if you add exta of any ingredient, that just makes the whole dish better. It is hard to mess this one up.

Ingredients

  • 32 oz. Frozen Cubed Hash Brown Potatoes
  • 16 oz. Sour cream
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 yellow one, diced
  • 2 cups Corn Flakes

Steps

  1. Thaw out hash browns
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl
  3. Coat a 9x13 inch casserole dish with butter
  4. Add casserole mixture
  5. Crush Cornflakes and with 2 tbpsp butter
  6. Add Corn Flakes to top of casserole
  7. Bake for 40-50 minutes at 350 degrees
