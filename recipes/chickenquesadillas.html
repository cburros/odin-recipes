Chicken Quesadillas

Description

My ex-wife made this version of chicken quesadillas. It is the best I have ever tried. I can't find a restaurant that makes it this way. The secret is both the picante and cream cheese. Together, they make an amazing sauce.

I am convinced that if I sold these on a food truck (along with churros), I'd make quite a bit of money. It is also important, but probably not necessary, to shred the chicken as well. This can easily be done with two forks. The idea is to hold the chicken up over a pot with one fork and to shred it using the second fork.

Ingredients

  • Two Baked Chicken Breasts, shredded
  • 1 cup Picante sauce
  • 1 block cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar chesse
  • 2 large flour tortillas

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the Picante and cream cheese
  2. Mix shredded chicken in with sauce mixture
  3. Place tortilla in a large skillet
  4. Layer cheese on one half of tortilla
  5. Add chicken mixutre on top of cheese
  6. Fold tortilla over chicken mixture
  7. Cook quesadilla on medium heat for 5 minutes or until Golden brown
  8. Flip tortilla, cook on other side until golden brown
